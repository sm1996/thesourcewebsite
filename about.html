The Source was essentially created with this statement in mind.

The brand is an ode to local artisans in emerging economies, giving them a platform to show the world what their country is really all about.

For the debut collection, we worked with local Rickshaw mistries in Dhaka, Bangladesh to create a series of raincoats and t-shirts.

The artisans are paid fairly for their artwork and given a share of the profit from future sales.
